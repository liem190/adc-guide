Reasons why Kai-Sa is the best ADC

  1. She's hot.

  2. Your W gives you two stacks of plasma. This makes your level 2 all in insane if you AA 3 times and W.
  3. If you make sure to position your champion properly, your isolated q does an insane amount of damage.
  4. If you're shit at CSing Kai-Sa q executes minions past a certain amount of hp

  5. You can animation cancel abilities with autoes. Just auto (then use ability). Applies to all abilities.
  6. If you auto then ult, you will auto attack 2 times in one second.
  7. You can W flash to reposition your character. This means you can surprise your enemy by W'ing past minions.
  8. Once you evolve E, you can pretend to move one direction, then e the other to juke them.
  9. You can play her AP

Follow this guide and you can play like this.
