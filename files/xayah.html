How to play Xayah

  1. Pay attention to your feathers during laning phase, cause on g whoever you're vsing won't. Easiest root of your life.
  2. Position your feathers in lane where you know your opponents will go (ie for cannon).
  3. Position your character right in front of your opponent and in between your feathers. In this way all the feathers will hit them for max damage.
  4. Following in from the previous point. You can e flash directly in front of your opponent to surprise them.

  5. You can also e, flash, gale force to increase distance like so.
  6. Only use your W when you're in auto attack range to maximise damage
  7. You can also Q and then E to have it pull back delayed.
